🎁 PM Pro Tips: Customize AI Tools Like a Boss

Stop using AI like Google. Here’s how to get strategic output.

🧠 ChatGPT / Claude

  • Go to ChatGPT → Settings → Custom Instructions: Fill in “What should ChatGPT know?” and “How should it respond?”
  • Turn on Memory: Let ChatGPT remember your role, preferences, and tone
  • Use real examples: Provide templates and real responses for it to learn from

📝 Otter.ai / Fireflies

  • Name your meetings: "Sprint Planning – Sept 22"
  • Mark actions: Say clearly: "Decision:..." or "Action:..."
  • Export clean transcript: and use AIpairwise to summarize or plan

📂 Notion / Asana

  • Paste your results: into project briefs or tasks
  • Break down tasks: with deadlines, owners, deliverables
  • Assign to team: Tag collaborators to take action
