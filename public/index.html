🛡️ AI Guardrails – ISO-Inspired Governance Pack

Practical best practices, fact-checking workflow, and minimal governance framework.

1) Scope & Purpose

Define approved AI tools, acceptable use cases, and responsibilities (Admin, Reviewer, User).

2) Data Security

  • No PII/financial/client confidential data in prompts unless you have proper legal basis & contracts (DPA/BAA).
  • Data minimization: share only what’s necessary; redact identifiers.
  • Encrypt and control access to AI-generated documents.

3) Privacy & Compliance

  • Map usage to applicable obligations (e.g., ISO/IEC 27001:2022 families, ISO/IEC 27701:2019 privacy themes).
  • Maintain logs of AI usage; define retention & deletion.
  • Human review required for external-facing content.

4) Accuracy & Fact-Check

  • Verify critical outputs against two independent sources; prefer authoritative references.
  • Request citations; archive links used for decisions.
  • Escalate suspected hallucinations or bias; document corrections.

5) Minimal Governance Checklist

  • Assign roles & approvals; publish the “approved tools” list.
  • Standardize prompt templates by use case.
  • Quarterly review of prompts, outputs, and incidents.
